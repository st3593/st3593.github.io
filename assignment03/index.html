WAYS OF SEEING

"I am an eye, a mechanical eye. I the machine [camera] show you a world the way only I can see it. I free myself for today and for ever from human immobility. I am in constant movement. ... Freed from the boundaries of time and space, I coordinate any and all points of the universe wherever I want them to be. My way leads towards the creation of a fresh perception of the world. Thus I explain in a new way, the world unknown to you."

- Dziga Vertov, Russian film director (1923)
